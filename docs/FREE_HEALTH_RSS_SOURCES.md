# Free Health News RSS Sources

This document lists the free health news RSS sources that have been added to the health category.

## Verified Free RSS Sources

### 1. **CDC (Centers for Disease Control and Prevention)**
- **RSS URL**: `https://tools.cdc.gov/api/v2/resources/media/132950.rss`
- **Description**: Public health news, disease prevention, health statistics, and public health guidelines from the U.S. government
- **Update Frequency**: 1 hour
- **License**: Public domain (U.S. government)

### 2. **Medical Xpress**
- **RSS URL**: `https://medicalxpress.com/rss-feed/health-news/`
- **Description**: Free medical and health news aggregator
- **Update Frequency**: 30 minutes
- **License**: Free for personal/non-commercial use

### 3. **News-Medical.net**
- **RSS URL**: `https://www.news-medical.net/rss/health-news.aspx`
- **Description**: Free medical news covering research, treatments, and health information
- **Update Frequency**: 30 minutes
- **License**: Free for personal/non-commercial use

### 4. **ScienceDaily Health**
- **RSS URL**: `https://www.sciencedaily.com/rss/health_medicine.xml`
- **Description**: Health and medicine news from scientific research
- **Update Frequency**: 30 minutes
- **License**: Free for personal/non-commercial use

### 5. **MedlinePlus**
- **RSS URL**: `https://medlineplus.gov/rss/all_healthnews.xml`
- **Description**: Health information from the National Library of Medicine (NIH)
- **Update Frequency**: 1 hour
- **License**: Public domain (U.S. government)

### 6. **FDA News**
- **RSS URL**: `https://www.fda.gov/AboutFDA/ContactFDA/StayInformed/RSSFeeds/FDANewsroom/rss.xml`
- **Description**: FDA health and safety news, drug approvals, recalls, and public health alerts
- **Update Frequency**: 1 hour
- **License**: Public domain (U.S. government)

## Total Health Sources

With these additions, the health category now has **11 free RSS sources**:
- 5 existing sources (WebMD, Healthline, Medical News Today, Mayo Clinic, NIH)
- 6 new sources (CDC, Medical Xpress, News-Medical.net, ScienceDaily Health, MedlinePlus, FDA)

## Usage Rights

All sources listed above are:
- **Free to use** for personal and non-commercial purposes
- **Public domain** (for government sources like CDC, NIH, FDA, MedlinePlus)
- **Free RSS feeds** (for commercial sources like Medical Xpress, News-Medical.net, ScienceDaily)

## Notes

- These RSS feeds are publicly available and do not require API keys
- All sources provide health-related content suitable for aggregation
- Government sources (.gov) are particularly reliable and free to use
- Server-side RSS fetching (via `/api/rss`) will help bypass CORS restrictions and rate limits
